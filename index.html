An Overview

Michael-Nice Foundation is a dream from the loss of a dear brother,and how he lived to help those in need. The organization was registered in 2018 in Houston Texas,USA.

The vision emanates from the sudden death of her beloved brother Angel Michael, who was a passionate advocate for the downtrodden,Widows and for Justice. The residue of his legacy will continually last through this mission.

We address social barriers to economic growth by providing financial assistance to alleviate sufferings of the less-privilege.
